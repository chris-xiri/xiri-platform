## Pre-Test Checklist

- [ ] Confirm device inventory and locations
- [ ] Verify access permissions and keys
- [ ] Pull prior test and repair records
- [ ] Pre-brief on known pressure or valve issues

## During-Test Checklist

- [ ] Capture device ID and result per unit
- [ ] Flag immediate failures and likely repair scope
- [ ] Record technician credentials and timestamps

## Post-Test Checklist

- [ ] Schedule repairs for failed devices same day
- [ ] Book retest window at time of repair dispatch
- [ ] Verify submission packet completeness
- [ ] Archive records in property compliance folder
