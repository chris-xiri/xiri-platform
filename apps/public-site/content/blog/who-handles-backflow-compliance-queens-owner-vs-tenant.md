## Why Responsibility Gets Confusing

In Queens mixed-use and multi-tenant properties, testing responsibility can get blurred between ownership, management, and tenant operations.

## Typical Responsibility Model

### Owner Responsibilities

- Maintain building-wide compliance program
- Ensure annual testing occurs
- Coordinate filing and record retention

### Tenant Responsibilities

- Provide access where devices are in leased areas
- Report plumbing changes affecting device risk
- Cooperate with scheduled testing windows

### Management Responsibilities

- Execute scheduling and vendor coordination
- Maintain compliance packet per property
- Escalate failures and reinspection workflow

## Lease Language and Operating Reality

Even when lease language allocates cost, building operations still need a single execution owner to avoid missed deadlines.
