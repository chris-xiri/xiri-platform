## Why Tenant SLAs Build Trust Faster

In multi-tenant buildings, owners lose trust when tenants report inconsistent cleanliness but no one can show objective service data.

## SLA Checklist

- Defined cleaning frequency by zone.
- Day porter coverage windows.
- Restroom and high-touch standards.
- Escalation contacts and response times.
- Missed-task correction deadlines.
- Documentation standards for completion proof.

## Communication Cadence

- Weekly summary to property management.
- Monthly quality and incident report for key tenants.
- Quarterly service review and scope adjustment.

## Metrics to Publish to Tenants

- On-time completion rate.
- Missed-task correction time.
- Supply stockout incidents.
- Inspection pass rate.
