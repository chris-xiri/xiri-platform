## You Received a Notice. Now What?

Treat the letter as a deadline-driven workflow. Your objective is to demonstrate active remediation quickly and complete a closed compliance cycle.

## Step 1: Confirm the Trigger

Identify whether the notice is for missed annual test, failed device, filing gap, or record mismatch.

## Step 2: Schedule Certified Testing Immediately

Reserve the earliest available slot and brief the tester on known site issues.

## Step 3: Pre-Authorize Repairs

If failure is likely, pre-authorize common repair ranges to avoid approval delays.

## Step 4: Execute Retest and Finalize Records

A passed retest is only useful when paired with complete documentation and confirmed submission.

## Step 5: Archive and Calendar Next Cycle

Close the current notice and schedule the next annual window immediately.
